FAQ

What you're probably wondering

My staff are older and not phone-savvy — will they manage?
The staff app has only 3 main actions — pick vehicle, pick service, mark done. Bilingual UI + large buttons, 1-hour training is enough. If anyone gets stuck later, we coach them via WhatsApp — no extra work for you.
What happens to my existing member records and point cards?
Data migration is included. Hand us your paper cards, Excel or member list — we clean and import everything; existing point balances are preserved. Customers lose nothing.
Do customers have to download an app to view points?
No. The customer portal is a web app (PWA) — send a WhatsApp link to open it; it can be added to the home screen and used like an app. No App Store, no APK, no friction even for older customers.
Can I trial first before deciding?
The Professional plan includes a free first month — once setup is done, you have 30 days to evaluate. Not happy? We refund the full setup fee. You lose only the data-migration time, no cash.
Is the data secure? Could revenue or customer data leak?
Data sits on Supabase in the Singapore region (PDPA-compliant) with daily auto-backups. Only you and the staff you authorize can log in — staff see only what they should, owners see everything. Even if our company disappears, you can export 100% of your data.
Can I edit my own packages and prices? My branches charge differently.
Yes. The admin panel lets you add or edit packages and prices yourself. Each branch can have its own price list — a sedan wash can be RM 25 in Austin and RM 22 in Skudai with no conflict.
I have 2 branches — do I need 2 subscriptions?
No. Professional supports up to 2 branches; Enterprise supports unlimited. Each branch has its own staff, revenue and stock reports — but one login covers them all.
Where in JB are you based? Can I reach you if something breaks?
We're right here in Johor Bahru. Instant WhatsApp replies, on-site visits when needed — that's our biggest difference from overseas SaaS.
